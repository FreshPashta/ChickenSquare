    About Chicken Square

    Chicken Square was designed to help new and experienced backyard chicken enthusiasts make sense of common ailments and to encourage best practices for home flock management.

    Chickens mask illness well, meaning that by the time you notice symptoms, careful intervention is often necessary.

    Important Disclaimer

    This app is not a diagnostic tool. It must not present definitive diagnoses and does not encourage risky at-home treatments. The information contained is compiled from standard backyard poultry manuals and guidelines.

    If you suspect an infectious disease (like Avian Influenza or Newcastle Disease) due to a sudden multiple die-off in your flock, you MUST report this to your state agricultural extension or poultry diagnostic lab.
